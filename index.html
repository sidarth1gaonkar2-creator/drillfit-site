FAQ

The quick answers.

What is DrillFit?
A fitness tracker with a drill-sergeant personality. Log your workouts, track nutrition (including meals from 38+ restaurants), and climb a military-style strength ranking as you get stronger.
Does DrillFit sync with Apple Health?
Yes. With your permission, DrillFit reads and writes data such as workouts, active energy, and body weight through Apple HealthKit. You control this anytime in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices, and your Health data is never used for advertising or sold.
Where is my data stored?
Your account and logs are stored securely using Google Firebase (authentication, database, and storage). See the Privacy Policy below for the full breakdown.
How does the strength ranking work?
Ranks are calculated from your lifts relative to your bodyweight, then mapped onto a five-phase military rank ladder from Private up to Sergeant Major of the Army.
How do I delete my account and data?
You can request full deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing support@drillfit.app. We'll remove your data from our systems.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DrillFit ("we", "us", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the DrillFit mobile application. By using DrillFit, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign up, we collect your email address (and basic profile info if you sign in with Google) to create and secure your account.
  • Fitness & nutrition data you enter. Workouts, sets, weights, body weight logs, food and meal entries, and goals that you record in the app.
  • Apple Health (HealthKit) data. With your explicit permission, we read and/or write data such as active energy, workouts, and body weight. This data is used only to power features inside the app. It is never used for advertising and is never sold.
  • Community content. Posts, comments, reactions, and follow relationships you create in the in-app community feed.
  • Diagnostics. Crash reports and basic performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics) to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

How we use your information

  • To provide core features: tracking, charts, rankings, nutrition logging, and the community feed.
  • To sync your data across your devices and back it up.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app reliability and performance.
  • To respond to your support requests.

Third-party services

DrillFit relies on the following services, each with its own privacy practices:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage, crash reporting) — stores your account and app data.
  • Apple HealthKit — on-device health data, accessed only with your permission.
  • Nutrition databases (USDA FoodData Central, Spoonacular, Open Food Facts) — when you search for foods, we send your search query to look up nutrition information. We do not send your personal account data to these services.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Content you choose to post in the community feed is visible to other users. Apart from the service providers listed above (who process data on our behalf), we do not share your personal data with third parties except where required by law.

Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing support@drillfit.app, and we will remove it from our systems.

Children's privacy

DrillFit is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Security

We use industry-standard measures, including the security features provided by Firebase, to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to keep your information safe.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
