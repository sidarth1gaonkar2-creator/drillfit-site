FAQ

What is DrillFit?
A fitness tracker with a drill-sergeant personality. Log your workouts, track nutrition (including meals from 38+ restaurants), and climb a military-style strength ranking as you get stronger.
Does DrillFit sync with Apple Health?
Yes. With your permission, DrillFit reads and writes data such as workouts, active energy, and body weight through Apple HealthKit. You control this anytime in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices, and your Health data is never used for advertising or sold.
How does the strength ranking work?
Ranks are calculated from your lifts relative to your bodyweight, then mapped onto a 10-rank military ladder from Private up to Sergeant Major of the Army.
What is the AI Coach?
The AI Coach answers training questions and can build workouts from your real lift history or adjust your nutrition goals — always with your confirmation before anything changes. It speaks in the drill-sergeant voice, but it won't change your data without a tap.
Where is my data stored?
Your account and logs are stored securely using Google Firebase (authentication, database, and storage). See the Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.
How do I delete my account and data?
You can request full deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing support@drillfit.com. We'll remove your data from our systems.
